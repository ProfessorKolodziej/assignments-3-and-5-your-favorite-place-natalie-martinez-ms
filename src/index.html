Land of Ooo

Our History

Embark on a magical journey through the enchanting Land of Ooo! Adventure awaits around every corner with our fantastical landscapes surer to capture your fancy. Will you stroll through the Gumdrop Gardens or traverse down the Marshmallow Mines? Perhaps embark on a quest through the treacherous, flaming paths of the Fire Kingdom! There is no shortage of magical mysteries in our treasured realm.

Top Attractions

Candy Kingdom

Immerse yourself in the sugary fueled delights of the Candy Kingdom. From the jawbreaker fields to the licorice bridges, every corner of this sweet treat realm is pure awesomeness. Keep an eye out for mischievous candy critters and get ready to have your taste buds rocked

Fire Kingdom

Whoa, dudes! Get ready to feel the heat in the Fire Kingdom – Flame Princess' fiery fortress of awesome! It's like stepping into a blaze-filled adventure where flames dance to their own rad rhythm.

Ice Kingdom

Mathematical, dude! The Ice Kingdom is a total frosty wonderland ruled by the Ice King. Get ready for some chilly adventures as we navigate through glaciers and snowy peaks. Watch out for the Ice King's freezing spells and his penguin sidekicks.

Travel Tips

  • Enchanted Snacks for Energy: Pack Rainbow Gum Drops and Cotton Candy Cloud Bites
  • Master Royal Transportation: Soar on a broomstick or ride your royal carriage through Ooo's diverse landscapes.
  • Learn the Lingo: Familiarize yourself with algebraic phrases for hidden quests and local connections.
  • Interact with the Locals: Chat with candy people, shape-shifting dogs, and mystical creatures, to unearth hidden gems, gain valuable insights, or even join an impromptu dance-off!
  • Stay Open to the Unexpected: Embrace Ooo's unpredictability, where ordinary moments turn into magical adventures.
